About

I work at Eneco Energy Trade, where I help building a streaming data analytics platform centered around Apache Kafka. Some of the projects I have worked on at Eneco are:

  • Landscaper, a layer on top of Helm for easy desired state in Kubernetes;
  • kafka-connect-twitter, a Twitter source/sink for Kafka Connect;
  • kafka-streams-plumber, script boring stream transformations through Lua.

As a Datamountaineer I’ve made:

  • go-schema-registry, a Go client and CLI for Confluent’s schema registry;
  • kafka-connect-tools, a CLI in Scala for Kafka Connect.
